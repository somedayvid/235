Music Time

Hello and welcome to Music Time! Click on any button to make sounds play. When changing sound settings please remember to close the sound first by clicking the button for the corresponding sound into the unpressed position!

Party Mode

Enabling party mode enables lots of flashing lights. Use at your own risk. Party mode is off by default. To activate first enable the button and then meet the requirement of producing a sick beat.
