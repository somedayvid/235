Untitled Dungeon Crawler

Concenpt

A 2d 1-bit dungeon crawler. The map will have various paths and the player will have a set of actions that they can take to try and get through the dungeon and survive.

Asethethics

One bit as mentioned previously, sounds will also probably be sort of classic 8 bit sounds, any music would probably utilize chiptune sounds a lot.

Genre

Adventure, dungeon crawler, card game

Platform

Desktop only

Story

The player is an adventurer and has to get through the dungeon to escape.

Gameplay

Player will have the choice to walk to various parts of the dungeon and resolve any obstacles that are in their way using their mouse to choose where they go and what they do.
