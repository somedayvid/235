About Me

Hi! My name is David Gao and I am a Game Design and Development and English Double Major. My double major allows me to pursue the creation of the things that I've enjoyed for most of my life, and hope to create, for others to enjoy too. Whether it be in video games, film, television, or books, I just want to acquire for myself the knowledge to put out something that I can be proud of. I'm hoping that this class will give me the skills to, on my own, create a space that showcases more of me. My time at RIT will hopefully pull me together enough to gain the skils to fulfill my dreams, and when I graduate I hope that I'll be creating for the rest of my life.
